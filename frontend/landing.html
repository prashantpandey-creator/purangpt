What does the Bhagavata Purana say about the nature of the Self (Atman)?
ॐ

The Bhagavata Purana describes the Ātman as eternal, self-luminous, and untouched by the modifications of the material mind. In Skandha 11, Śrī Kṛṣṇa instructs Uddhava that the Self is like a witness — it illuminates all experiences yet is never contaminated by them, just as fire illuminates without being affected by what it burns.

आत्मा नित्यः शुद्धो बुद्धो मुक्त-स्वभावः।
न जायते म्रियते वा विपश्चित् ॥
