# THE AWAKENER — Art Direction (the "feel," derived from the corpus)

> Not a mood-board guess. Every aesthetic rule here traces to a decode key in
> `guruji_ram.json` or a graph fact. This is the visual vow a renderer (UE5 etc.)
> obeys so the world LOOKS as true as the lore is. Competitors building "dark
> fantasy reimaginings" cannot reproduce this — they lack the decode.

## The one law: the world is lit from WITHIN

The corpus is saturated with a single, coherent aesthetic of light — and it is NOT
torchlit-medieval (European fantasy) nor shadowed-dark (Black Myth: Wukong). It is
**radiance**. Cited:

- *"unbearable brilliance of Time: the flame of all flames, color of the sun"*
- *"thousands of suns: the immense brilliance of the Supreme Self in samadhi"*
- *"Golden aura: the brilliant Time appearing as an aura around the heads of the
  Time-Conscious"*
- *"Time is the brilliance that illuminates sun, moon, fire"* (na tad bhāsayate sūryaḥ)
- *"Moon: a partial glimpse of the light of Time"*; *jyotsna: moonlight*
- *"Tejas: the inner light of awareness"*; *"Splendor, brilliance, vigor: signs of
  the Supreme Time"*

**Rule: gold is not decoration — it is the visible body of consciousness.** Light
sources read as inner/divine, not fire. Skies and water carry the brilliance.
Sacred/awakened beings have a faint golden aura (literal corpus image). The register
is luminous, warm, gold-saturated, radiant — never grimdark.

## Palette (corpus-named)

- **Gold / saffron** — consciousness, dharma, the kshatriya register, Time's light.
- **White** — purity of the senses (*"white horses: the pure, controlled senses"*),
  the Brahmin register (quietude/knowledge).
- **Moon-silver** — *jyotsna*, the partial glimpse; for night/cooler beats.
- Deep dusk violets/umbers only as the GROUND the brilliance falls on, never the
  dominant key. Darkness exists to make the inner light legible.

## How a renderer uses this

- Lighting rig: dominant warm inner-radiance key from above/within; minimal hard
  shadow; bloom on gold surfaces (used sparingly — it's brilliance, not neon).
- Material direction: gold/brass/saffron as the hero materials; white stone/cloth;
  water and sky tinted with the key.
- Auras: a subtle golden rim on awakened/divine beings (corpus-literal).
- Tie every set-dressing decision back to a decode key or a graph fact; flag
  invented forms as [REASONED EXTENSION] in the asset notes, same discipline as the
  codex's [NEEDS-INGESTION].
